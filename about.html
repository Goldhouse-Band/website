About Us

We are a group of musical misfits bound together by our love for untamed rhythms, the raw energy of folk storytelling, and the rebellious spirit of punk rock.

Our journey began on the shores of California in 2021 as a duo of Riley and Jared playing live shows. The following year guitarist Jon joined with Jared moving from guitar to bass. The band was a three piece performance until Jeff followed suit on the drums. In late 2023 the project moved to Arizona shaking things up with Daniel on guitar, Anessa on bass, and AJ on drums.
